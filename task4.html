About Me

I’m Narendra Kumar Raavi, a UI/UX Developer and Frontend Engineer based in Franklin Park, New Jersey. I recently completed my Master of Science in Information Technology from the University of Cincinnati, where I maintained a GPA of 3.96/4.00. I also hold a Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University.

Currently, I work at Sunus LLC, where I collaborate with UX designers to develop responsive web pages and create intuitive user interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My role involves designing wireframes and prototypes and ensuring UI consistency while optimizing performance and conducting testing.

Prior to this, I worked at Arohak, where I developed dynamic web applications with Python and Django and focused on enhancing user experience through responsive design. I also led student projects and mentored peers during my time as president of IEEE & IETE camp, demonstrating my leadership and problem-solving skills.

I’ve also completed several certifications, including Google UX Design and Full-Stack Web Development, which have broadened my knowledge and expertise in web technologies and design.

My technical skills span various programming languages and web technologies, including JavaScript, Python, React.js, and Bootstrap. I’m passionate about creating high-quality user experiences and continually improving my skills to stay at the forefront of technology.
